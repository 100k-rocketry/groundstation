THE GROUNDSTATION TEAM

We love rockets

In June 2017, the OSU chapter of the AIAA will launch a rocket in the Mojave Desert. The software team will develop the tracking software that receives telemetry from the rocket during its flight and displays the data in real-time for the entire OSU rocketry team. This software must be flexible, robust, and accurate in order to ensure that the variety of engineers on the project all receive the data they need on launch data. This document will give an overview of the problem, the software team's proposed solution, and describe how the success of the project will be measured.
